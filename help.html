Instructions

Welcome to Preptastic! Here are some instructions to get you started:

First, you want to add some recipes to the recipes page. You can do so by navigating to the Recipes page and then clicking the "Create Recipe" button.

Second, you want to navigate back to the main page containing the calendar and start planning when you want to make certain recipes! Simply fill in the add meal form and then click the "Add Meal" button.

Once you've got some recipes on the calendar, feel free to navigate to the Ingredients page to start planning what ingredients you have to get. Simply add each ingredient for the recipes you've planned to the Ingredients page. If you already have a particlar ingredient in your pantry then feel free to check it off the list!
