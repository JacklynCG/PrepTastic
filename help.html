Instructions

Welcome to Preptastic! Here are some instructions to get you started:

First, you want to add some recipes to the recipes page. You can do so by navigating to the Recipes page and then clicking the...

Second, you want to navigate back to the main page containing the calendar and start planning when you want to make certain recipes! Simply select one of the three meal times listed on each day to get started.

Once you've got some recipes on the calendar, feel free to navigate to the Ingredients page to start planning what ingredients you have to get. Simply add each ingredient for the recipes you've planned to the Ingredients page and then add to a specific grocery shopping list based on how you choose to organize them. If you already have a particlar ingredient in your pantry then feel free to check it off the list!

Frequently Asked Questions

How much data is saved on this website?

The Preptastic system saves up to a year's worth of data.

How do I get started with using the Preptastic website?

Simply add recipes to the recipes page and then start planning when you will utilize those recipes by adding them to a specific day on the calendar on the homepage.
